What Does “Studying Abroad” Mean at UTokyo?

The University of Tokyo offers several types of study abroad programs, mainly including:

  1. Short-term programs, lasting a few weeks
  2. University-wide student exchange programs, lasting one semester to one year
  3. Faculty or department-based programs, lasting one semester to one year depending on faculty

Todai Study Abroad GoGo mainly supports the university-wide student exchange program, USTEP, which attracts around 300 applicants each year.

Applications are typically open twice a year, in autumn and spring, and students are selected through an internal screening process.
