Events

What we organize

At Todai Study Abroad GoGo, we organize events such as:

  • Networking opportunities with students interested in studying abroad and returnees
  • Online information sessions about exchange programs
  • Other opportunities to learn about studying abroad

Below is a list of our events in 2025.

2025 Event Highlights

May

In-person

Spring Networking Event

Every spring, we hold an in-person networking event where students interested in studying abroad can interact with returnees, accepted students, and other applicants.

Through face-to-face interaction, this event aims to help prospective students resolve their questions and concerns, while also providing opportunities to learn about application procedures and meet future peers for their study abroad journey.

With over 100 participants every year, this is GoGo’s largest event.

July

In-person

Accepted Students Networking Event

This is an in-person networking event where students who have been accepted to study abroad can interact with returnees and other accepted students.

Students who are about to study abroad gather to connect with each other and with returnees, allowing them to resolve real concerns and questions that arise just before departure.

September

Online

Pre-Application Online Info Session

We hold an online information session via Zoom for students who are planning to apply for study abroad.

Participants can listen to explanations from returnees about their host universities and ask questions directly.

In-person

Pre-Application Networking Event

We hold an in-person event where students applying for the autumn university-wide exchange program can interact with each other.

This event provides an opportunity for applicants to ask questions to returnees and connect with fellow applicants.

Online

Last-Minute Discussion Session

Just before the autumn application deadline, members of Todai Study Abroad GoGo host an online discussion session via Zoom to answer applicants’ questions.

October

In-person

Study Abroad Info Session for Komaba Students

This is an in-person event mainly for first- and second-year students, designed to help them gain a better understanding of what studying abroad involves.

For students who are interested in studying abroad but are unsure where to start, we provide presentations by returnees as well as workshops to help them take their first step.
